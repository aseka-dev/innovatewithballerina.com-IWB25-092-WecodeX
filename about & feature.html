About RoomSync

RoomSync is a smart roommate management tool designed to make shared living easier, more organized, and stress-free. Whether you’re sharing an apartment, hostel, or dorm, RoomSync helps you keep track of chores, bills, and schedules in one simple platform.

Our mission is to bring harmony, fairness, and simplicity into everyday shared living. No more arguments about who cleaned last, or confusion over expenses RoomSync keeps everything transparent and balanced.
